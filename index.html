    Resource Pack Music Creator

    How it works

    1. Upload your custom audio track (convert to .ogg for Minecraft compatibility).

    2. Choose which vanilla disc you want to replace.

    3. Click Generate. We will build the correct 1.8.8 pack structure (assets/minecraft/sounds/records/) and download a ready-to-use ZIP file.

    Note: Minecraft requires audio files to be in .ogg format. You can use an online converter if you have an .mp3 or .wav file.
